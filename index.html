Buyer FAQs

Common questions from our international clients

We have no minimum order quantity. Whether you're launching a capsule collection or scaling a full streetwear line, we work with you at any volume. Contact us to discuss your needs.
We offer free fabric sampling so you can feel the quality before committing to an order. Garment sample production takes 7 days after design and fabric confirmation.
We accept 30% advance via T/T (bank transfer) with the 70% balance payable before shipment. L/C at sight is available for orders above $10,000. PayPal is accepted for sample payments.
We ship FOB (Surat / Mumbai port), CIF to your destination port, and Ex-Works from our factory. We work with all major freight forwarders and can help coordinate logistics on your behalf.
